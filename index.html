--- layout: default --- Unity WebGL Player | Space Shooter

Controls

  • Use Arrow Keys or WASD to move
  • Use the left ctrl or mouse left click to shoot
  • Toggle Weapons with Numbers 1-4
  • *Note Weapon 5 (Homing) Does Not Work

How to Play

  • Scroll down to bottom of page
  • Hit the maximize button
  • Use the controls to destroy the asteroids and enemy ships
  • You may need to intall Unity WebGL Chrome extension
  • Enjoy!
